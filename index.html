Who are we?

GameHub is an online shop where users can purchase computer games. Games could either be used games, they could be brand new games or they could be pre-ordered games.

We are a competitively priced website with a huge range of games.

Why choose us?

For those selling a used game, we offer the opportunity to regain some of the money back from games which you have finished playing.

For those buying games, we offer the opportunity to support a fellow gamer for a significantly lower price than an all-new game would initially cost.

You can also buy brand new games, or pre-order not yet released games.
